History


1989

Our founder, Rohan Sharma started this now million-dollar company as a microprocessor manufacturing company. He did not access to the luxury of the then modern computer chips.The first microprocessor, Malay 100, was one of the fastest chips made by anyone in India in the 1990s. However, Rohan Sharma wasnt one to settle and he started to diverge the products of the company to making computers in whole.

2001-2004

In 2001, a revolution changed the company from its very roots. A new CEO, Richard Hendricks was appointed by the board. It was decided that since tere were a lot of manufacturers of chips, the company will shift its focus on making monitors. As time passed, monitors turned to TVs and in 2004, the first Pixnet TV was launched. This turned out to be a blessing for the company and its sales and stocks sky-rocketed.

2015

Afterwards, Pixnet turned vision towards new products a lot frequently. Pixnet turned into a global tech manufacturer and research company. It started employing more and more people to keep up with the demand of the products. For the past few years, our engineers have put the company's experience in processors and displays to make something incredible. To make something ahead of its time. The third generation of our phone line- The Tesla T3
